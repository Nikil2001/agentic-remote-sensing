🌍 About the Project

This application leverages U-Net-based deep learning segmentation to classify land cover from satellite imagery, providing precise land type distribution reports and intelligent summaries powered by Google Gemini LLM. It also enables comparison of two temporal satellite images to detect changes in land use such as urbanization, deforestation, or agricultural expansion. Designed for research and institutional use, including applications within ISRO, academia, and geospatial intelligence.
